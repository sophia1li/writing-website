About

Sophia Li is a Harvard medical student, writer, and journalist curious about what makes us human. She studied English and Biology at Yale and received the John Hersey Prize for responsible reportage. Her work spans science writing, investigative journalism, and creative nonfiction. She also currently edits nonfiction for the Intima.

When she’s not writing, she’s likely—running, crocheting, sketching—cycling between the many hobbies she’s picked up from her friends.
